Infrastructure
The style of the buildings is unique with large rectangular and linear structures dotted around the 32 acre campus with large courtyards. It has been designed keeping in mind the climatic conditions, still connecting the past with modern and contemporary design. The concept of the JU building and landscape is to create an environment with liveliness, as a playground for dreams and a place for inspiration to the students. The campus is built to bring experience of belongingness which truly represents the character of the University.

Hostel Life
On-campus residential facility for students has been very thoughtfully designed and planned. Each unit in the JU Hostel Complex allows privacy and personal space to a student while giving a chance to dwell together and foster comradeship. .
